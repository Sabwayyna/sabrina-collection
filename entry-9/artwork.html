Play Ambience Sound In Reflection

47” x 24” // Acrylic on canvas, Digital Manipulation - 2019 to 2023

Text Size Example

Three years ago marked the inception of this acrylic painting, a humble attempt to encapsulate an extraordinary, albeit seemingly impossible, depiction of the night sky. Back then, my artistic skills struggled to align with the ambitious vision I envisioned. Frustration mounted as progress lagged, eventually leading to the decision to set aside the incomplete piece. It became a dormant testament to the gap between aspiration and execution. Over the course of the intervening three years, a transformative journey unfolded. Armed with an evolved perspective, refined blending techniques, and a nuanced understanding of color, I revisited the dormant dreamscape. This time, I was equipped not only with enhanced technical skills but also with a renewed sense of determination and perseverance. In reclaiming and reviving the original vision onto the canvas, I introduced a symbolic element—a boat navigating through the reflective cerulean water. This addition served as a metaphor for my journey, mirroring my resilience and self-improvement. The tranquil waters reflected not only the sky above but also the personal growth and perseverance that navigated the passage of time. The completion of this piece is a testament to the transformative power of time and dedication. What was once deemed "impossible" in the initial stages of this artwork is now a vivid representation of the expansive possibilities that unfold between the commencement and completion of a creative endeavor. It stands as a reminder that our perception of what is achievable is often constrained by our current vantage point and can dramatically shift with time and growth.

Within the carousel on the left, a compelling chronicle unfolds, meticulously capturing the nuanced progression of my painting over a span of three transformative years—from its inception in 2019 to its culmination in 2023. This digital archive serves as an intimate reflection of my artistic journey, revealing the ebb and flow of creative impulses and technical prowess that have shaped the canvas. The first sub-finish, marking the initial completion in the early stages, bears the imprint of a moment of artistic struggle. Faced with the formidable challenge of aligning my vision with the tangible execution, a temporary sense of defeat prompted me to halt progress. This juncture signifies not only a pause in the artistic process but a pivotal moment of introspection, where the realization of the gap between aspiration and realization prompted a deeper exploration of my creative vision. So starting my revision, I wanted to plan carefully on how I would paint back into this artwork, something I have never done before. This the process for the initial ideas for my second revision.
The narrative unfolds into a second phase, resonating with the period designated for portfolio building. Here, armed with newfound painting skills and an enriched artistic sensibility, I revisited the dormant canvas. The second sub-finish became a testament to my resilience and growth as an artist. It marked a renaissance, where the fusion of honed skills and a refined artistic perspective breathed new life into the painting, transforming it into a vibrant expression of my evolving creative identity. The final chapter of this visual diary unfolds in 2023, coinciding with a class project that prompted a fresh perspective. In this iteration, digital manipulation emerged as a tool to extend the boundaries of traditional mediums. The third sub-finish encapsulates a harmonious synthesis of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary technology, allowing me to revisit the initial finished painting and infuse it with newfound layers of depth and complexity. The carousel stands not only as a temporal archive but as a testament to the iterative nature of the artistic process. Each sub-finish represents a unique chapter in my artistic evolution, marked by setbacks, rejuvenation, and a seamless integration of traditional and contemporary techniques. It is a visual record of my growth, a testament to the perpetual dialogue between imagination and execution that defines the trajectory of my artistic journey.
