Play Ambience Sound In Reflection

47” x 24” // Acrylic on canvas, Digital Manipulation - 2019 to 2023

Three years ago marked the beginning of my first large-scale acrylic painting, a humble attempt to encapsulate an extraordinary, seemingly impossible depiction of the night sky. Back then, my artistic skills struggled to align with the ambitious vision I envisioned. Frustration mounted as I was progressing slowly into the painting, and eventually I gave up, and set aside the incomplete piece. It became evident that there was a gap between aspiration and execution. Over the course of three years as I improved with painting and with an evolved perspective, I revisited this dreamscape. This time, I had not only with improved technical skills but also with a renewed sense of determination and perseverance.

In reclaiming and reviving my original vision onto the canvas, I added a symbolic element: a boat navigating through the reflective cerulean water. This addition served as a metaphor for my journey, mirroring my journey and self-improvement. The completion of this piece is the result of gradual time and dedication. What was once deemed "impossible" for me is now a vivid representation of the possibilities. It stands as a reminder that my perception of what is achievable is often constrained by what I believe is impossible.

The carousel shows the progression of my painting over a span of three years—from its start in 2019 to its end in 2023. This archive serves as a reflection of my artistic journey, revealing the highs and downs of my creative impulses and technical improvement that have shaped the canvas. When I first gave up in 2019, it was in a moment of artistic struggle. I was faced with the challenge of aligning my vision with a tangible execution, a temporary sense of defeat prompted me to stop painting. This problem signifies not only a pause in the painting process but a pivotal moment of self-doubt, where I realized the gap between aspiration and my creative vision. So starting my revision in 2020, I wanted to plan carefully on how I would paint back into this artwork, a process I have never done before. This the process for the initial ideas for my second revision.

Into a second phase of my revision, with newfound painting skills, I revisited the canvas for fun. During this re-painting, it showed my growth as an artist and also my artistic perspective. I aimed at transforming this into a vibrant expression of my evolving creative identity.

The final chapter of painting also is revised in 2023, with digital painting experience that prompted a fresh perspective. In this iteration, digital manipulation emerged as a tool for me to extend the boundaries of traditional mediums. I painted on top, adding small details such as more stars and colors to enhance the depth of the painting. For expanding the original painting into a landscape (for the sake of this entry), using AI, I expanded the borders of the painting. The third revision encapsulates a connection of traditional painting and contemporary technology, allowing me to revisit the initial finished painting and infuse it with newfound layers of depth and complexity.

This long painting process stands as an archive of my artistic process. Each sub-finish represents a unique chapter in my artistic evolution, marked by setbacks, improvements, and learning new mediums and technology. It is a visual record of my past imagination and different periods of execution that define the trajectory of my artistic journey.
