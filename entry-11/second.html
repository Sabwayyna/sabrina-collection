Cyclic Dread

My First Attempt in Animation

Duration: 1:11, Made in 2023

In this first-person animated experience, the viewer is thrust into a disconcerting scenario, awakening in a dimly lit, unfamiliar room. Startled and disoriented, the viewer navigates the obscure space, with only a faint glimmer of distant lights illuminating a long, foreboding hallway.

As the viewer cautiously advances, the lights lining the corridor flicker ominously, casting eerie shadows. With each passing moment, the lights sequentially extinguish, plunging the hallway into an increasing abyss of darkness. An unsettling tension builds as the viewer becomes acutely aware of their vulnerability in the encroaching blackness.

Suddenly, the furthest light reignites, revealing an otherworldly, alien-like entity beneath its glow. The viewer's initial confusion transforms into a visceral realization of imminent danger. Panic sets in, and the viewer instinctively flees, sprinting through the dim corridor in an attempt to escape the encroaching entity. However, the entity proves relentless, chasing the viewer with an otherworldly speed that defies comprehension. As the pursuer gains ground, the viewer's fear intensifies, heightening the sense of urgency. The inevitable confrontation looms as the entity catches up, culminating in a heart-pounding climax. In a swift and unsettling turn of events, the entity bites, and the screen fades to black, leaving the viewer to contemplate the ominous encounter in the lingering darkness.
