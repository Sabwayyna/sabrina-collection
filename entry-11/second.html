My First Attempt in Animation

Duration: 1:11, Made in 2023

In this first-person animation, the viewer is put into a disconcerting scenario, awakening in a dimly lit, unfamiliar room. Startled and disoriented, the viewer navigates the obscure space, with only a faint glimmer of distant lights illuminating a long, foreboding hallway.

As the viewer cautiously advances, the lights lining the corridor flicker ominously, casting eerie shadows. With each passing moment, the lights sequentially extinguish, plunging the hallway into an increasing abyss of darkness. An unsettling tension builds as the viewer becomes acutely aware of their vulnerability in the encroaching blackness.↓Scroll for more content↓

*Process Images

All frames were drawn by hand and edited together after shooting the images as stop motion. I used a simple color palette of dark reds and black-white, allowing viewers to be more focused on when color do appear, emphasizing the danger of the monster. As this was my first attempt in animation, I learned as I drew each frames. I aimed to use this learning process to my advantage, having simpler animation begin in the beginning, such as the water drop, then the blinking eye, then finally the monster. Overall, I am proud of this animation and I aim to explore more on this subject through my ever-changing and evolving learning process.
