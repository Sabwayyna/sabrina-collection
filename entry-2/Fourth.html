Disrupted Reality
In the final subpart, the personal imageries have transformed into a darkened, chaotic, and ghostly impressions of the past. Elements of the collage are barely recognizable, resembling the once-vivid moments.
It's a disconcerting sensation: The discomfort of knowing that a significant part of my life is now 'missing' is palpable. This subpart leaves the viewer with a poignant reflection on the transitory nature of life and the poignant complexity of nostalgia, where the act of remembering is intricately tied to the inevitability of forgetting.
---
This concluding art piece serves as a visual encapsulation of the chaos and gradual fading of my childhood memories, an embodiment of the transformation that has unfolded over the years. As I approach my 19th birthday, it becomes increasingly evident that my childhood, once vivid and familiar, has evolved into an environment I seldom revisit. The memories are now obscure, fragmented, and, in some instances, entirely forgotten.
Conclusion
This artistic journey transports me back to my early days in China, a time before the borders of my existence expanded to encompass the Canadian landscape. Shanxi, a small town, is the backdrop to these recollections, where everything seems to have been preserved as a hazy, dream-like montage. Whether within the four walls of a classroom, the confines of my bedroom, or the rustic charm of the neighborhood park, these memories are endowed with a certain indistinct quality.
Nostalgia, for me, has always been an enigmatic emotion, characterized by a delicate equilibrium of sweetness and melancholy. It hints at a discomforting sense of the unknown, fostering an air of mystery that has long intrigued me. It is precisely this beguiling sentiment that I aspire to encapsulate through the medium of collage, a format that, by its very nature, involves layering.
---
My objective is not only to capture my own experiences and emotions about how I feel about "nostalgia", but to extend an invitation to viewers on a shared exploration of mystifying and complicated feeling of nostalgia.
